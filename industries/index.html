Industries Served

SHATRACO Corporation provides sourcing and procurement services to companies in many industries around the globe.

Highly specialized in procurement services, SHATRACO is dedicated to helping our customers improve their bottom line and efficiencies. Our highly trained team has a proven track record of meeting our customers’ sourcing and procurement performance needs and for shortening the supply chain.

SHATRACO is proud to provide custom services to a number of Fortune 500 and Tier-One companies, in order to meet their very specific procurement needs.
