Value Added Solutions

Sharafco Trading Co‘s “bottom line benefits” improve our clients’ supply chain by providing cost-effective product sourcing solutions. SHATRACO understands the science behind product sourcing and delivers solutions that are on-time and within budget.

Sharafco Trading Co acts as an extension of our clients’ purchasing departments, providing sourcing and procurement of products for their manufacturing plants. The hundreds of quality manufacturers that SHATRACO works with allow us to procure and source products regionally and on a global scale.
